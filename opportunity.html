What I'm asking for, exactly

The part worth reading twice.

What it's based on
A specific system for mapping how a person is built. It's detailed, it's particular, and it isn't what you'd expect. I'll walk you through it on the call, before you commit to anything.
What gets recorded
Two short interviews with each person — about 30 minutes before we start, and 30 minutes after we finish. The sessions themselves are not recorded. Nobody works honestly on a camera, and honest is the whole point.
Who sees the recordings
Only me. They're working material, not content. Nothing from them is published in any form without your written approval.
What gets published
A written account of the engagement. You read it and approve it before it goes anywhere. Your name, your company, and identifying details are changed unless you tell me otherwise.
What you can stop
All of it, at any point, including after you've already said yes. Withdraw approval and nothing gets published; ask and the recordings are deleted. You don't need a reason.
What it costs
Nothing, and there's nothing waiting at the end of it. No proposal, no upsell, no follow-on engagement in the wings.
